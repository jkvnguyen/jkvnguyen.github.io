3D Affine Transformations


What I did:

Extended a program to steer a 3D spaceship by applying a series of affine transformations. Added interactivity where holding down left click while moving the mouse moves the camera such that the user can observe the ship from multiple directions.

  • S          decreases the velocity of the spaceship in the z-direction (forward)
  • Space Bar    resets the camera position to original location (0, 0, -5)
