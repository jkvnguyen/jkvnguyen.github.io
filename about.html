About Me


Hi, I'm Jeremy, a game designer and self-taught ux designer. Formerly an AR developer, I enjoy designing and developing experiences that brings out the best and joy in people.

Currently, I have a post-graduate in Game Design from George Brown College and a Bachelor of Arts in Digital Media at York University.

When I'm not deciding a font-face on Figma, you'll find me in the kitchen cooking something bougie or playing Magic: The Gathering with my friends. I am a big Toronto Raptors fan and lover of all things fantasy and Brandon Sanderson.
