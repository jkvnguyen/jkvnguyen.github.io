summary

A personal project to familiarize myself in Unreal Engine 5, Windrunner is a stealth survival game where the objective is to gather materials and escape a prison camp.

Game is inspired and based on Brandon Sanderson’s Stormlight Archive’s The Way of Kings.


Core Features

  • Survival mechanics with hunger/food buffs, crafting armor/bridge upgrades
  • Stealth mechanics during certain times with the ability to escape. Craftable uprades improve ability to escape.
  • Character stats that improve survivability.
  • Timed events to gather supplies before bridge runs. Successful bridge runs decrease number of guards and improve character stats.

Story

Based on Brandon Sanderson’s Stormlight Archive’s The Way of Kings, play as Kaladin during his struggles as a bridge runner. Kaladin is a slave who was sold as a bridge runner who supports the Alethi army during raids against the Parshendi. Bridge runners, like the name implies, run bridges due to landscaping that requires bridges to be set between the chasms. Bridge runners provide the Alethis with transportation in battle. However, as such, they are always targeted by Parshendi arrows and are often considered sacrifices during battle.


Target Audience

Fans of survival games and high fantasy themes.


Level Design

Luckily, there is a sketch of a map of Sadeas' camp in the book, where Kaladin was kept captive, so I was able to design a level based on this. I quickly translated this map to my own to add a legend and placement of where I want enemy AIs to spawn/traverse.
