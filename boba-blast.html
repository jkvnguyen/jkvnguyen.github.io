Boba Blast



Context

A game jam project I worked on at George Brown College where I worked as part of a small team to create a bubble tea inspired party game.


Milky Bubble Battle Boiz





Overview

Boba Blast is a casual multiplayer game where the objective is to be the last person standing. Play as one of the bubble tea inspired characters and use bubble tea toppings and attacks to knock friends out for the win.


Selling Points

  • Bubble tea themed
  • Cute and playful art style
  • Modern vibes w/ lofi
  • Unique combination of casual, shooter, and platformer


Game Features

  • Bubble tea toppings used as platforms to stand atop
  • Destructible platforms that deteriorate over a period of time
  • Third-person shooter where unique platforms can be used as different weapons to knock off opponents
  • Multiplayer mode of up to 4 players
  • Different games mode: Team, FFA, Custom
  • Fun bubble tea themed characters with different skin cosmetics
