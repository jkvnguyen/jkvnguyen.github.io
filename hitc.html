Overview

Worked freelance as a UI Programmer, where I developed many of the game’s UI systems and assisted with UX design.

Synopsis

17-year-old Hope Song returns home from school to find her parents missing. Unable to find answers for their strange disappearance, she digs into their case herself. Using her observant eye as an artist to piece together clues, Hope uncovers a dark world that lies beneath the surface of her peaceful urban life.

Hope in the City is a non-linear narrative mystery game from the team behind the 2023 GDC Pitch Winner Sky of Tides and the Annie Award-nominated Eternal Spring, Canada’s first animated feature chosen as the Best International Feature Film entry at the Academy Awards®.

What I Did:

  • UI Development: Implemented functioning UI elements for game menus, inventory, and events.
  • UI Animations: Added animations to unlock-able events applying filter masks for smooth transitions.
  • UX Flow: Assured UX flow and player usabity functioned smoothly to accompany the game's onboarding processes and game mechanics.
  • R&D: Researched and developed methods for page flip animations that masked page contents.
